Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2026

At Canwinn Care, we take your privacy and medical information security incredibly seriously. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, process, and protect your personal data when you use our web platform and clinical services.

1. Data We Collect

We log basic personal identifiable information (PII) such as your Name, Age, Email Address, and Phone Number. We also strictly safeguard Protected Health Information (PHI) like medical records, diagnostics, and prescriptions generated during telemedicine sessions or physical visits.

2. How We Use Data

  • To allocate you to the correct specialists efficiently.
  • To maintain your electronic medical history tracking for continuous care.
  • To dispatch automatic appointment scheduling alerts.

3. Data Security

All sensitive HTTP traffic is fully encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols. Protected Medical constraints rely on stringent HIPAA compliant architecture within our database layer, guaranteeing secure data transmission directly out of the application.
